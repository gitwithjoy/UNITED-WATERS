Meet Olawale Oladejo CEO, United Water Seekers Ltd. Professional Geologist | Water Exploration Expert Olawale Oladejo is a seasoned geologist and the visionary CEO of United Water Seekers Ltd., a company committed to delivering sustainable water solutions through scientific precision and innovation. A proud graduate of the Federal University of Technology, Akure (FUTA), he holds a degree in Geology and has spent the past years transforming his academic foundation into impactful field expertise. With a deep understanding of hydrogeology, environmental studies, and subsurface mapping, Olawale leads his team in locating, assessing, and managing groundwater resources across Nigeria. His passion for solving water accessibility challenges in rural and urban communities has made United Water Seekers a trusted name in borehole drilling and water resource consultancy. Driven by integrity, accuracy, and a commitment to environmental stewardship, Olawale continues to push the boundaries of geoscience to meet the growing demand for clean and reliable water.
